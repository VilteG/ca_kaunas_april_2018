Kylo Ren

Homeworld:Chandrila

Human Male

Kylo Ren, a Force-sensitive human male, was a dark side warrior who ruled the First Order as Supreme Leader during its war with the Resistance. Given the name Ben Solo, he was born in 5 ABY, one year after the Battle of Endor, on the planet Chandrila to Princess Leia Organa and General Han Solo, whose busy lives left Ben with feelings of abandonment. He was a scion of the Skywalker bloodline on his mother's side, and therefore possessed raw strength in the Force which ultimately attracted the attention of a dark side practitioner known as Supreme Leader Snoke.
